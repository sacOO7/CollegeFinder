All India Shree Shivaji Memorial Society’s College of Engineering (AISSMS CoE), Shivajinagar

 

Location:

All India Shree Shivaji Memorial Society’s College of Engineering (AISSMS CoE) is located right in the heart of the city, i.e., Shivajinagar. Its a mere 10 minute walk from Pune railway station and has excellent accessibility from all parts of the city, be it buses or trains.

 

Infrastructure:

The college building reminds you of the Hogwarts school from the famous Harry Potter series. It is dark and cool inside, and there is a good possibility of you losing your way although the staircases don’t move in this case. Labs are big but a some makeover is required as all the updated instruments are not available. Hostel facility is provided only for girls.

 

Fees:

Rs. 72,450/ year

 

Faculty:

The teaching at All India Shree Shivaji Memorial Society’s College of Engineering (AISSMS CoE) is good but can become boring at times. English communication is said to be a little problematic. “You have taken the test tomorrow” quotes one of the students as he tries to mimic his professor.

 

Attendance:

“Not necessary” says one of the AISSMS CoE student. No problems faced up to 20-30%. However, practical attendance is important.

 

Crowd:

Not good not bad. Typically what you can expect from an engineering college. Boys to girls ratio is around 70:30.

 

Hangouts:

Inox Multiplex, Mangla cinema hall, Pune Central mall, Sai corner and Paanchali restaurant. Neera outside the college and the local tapri are one of the favourites.

 

Canteen:

“Sucks..!!”, says one of the students, not replying to any further questions on the canteen.

 

Festivals:

Shivanjali is the cultural festival of All India Shree Shivaji Memorial Society’s College of Engineering (AISSMS CoE)  held in 1st week of February. ET is the technical festival, held around the same time.

 

Placements:

Around 65% of the students get placed. Accenture is major recruiter. Placements are much better compared to the sister institute AISSMS IoIT.

Verdict:

You can expect good overall development out here, since, more than attending lectures you would be engaged in other extracurricular activities.
